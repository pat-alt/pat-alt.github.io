Main

Patrick Altmeyer

Researching Trustworthy Artificial Intelligence (AI) for Finance and Economics

I am an economist by background with an interest in cross-disciplinary research on the intersection of Trustworthy AI and Financial Economics. For my PhD in Trustworthy AI, I currently focus on Counterfactual Explanations and Probabilisitic Machine Learning under supervision of Cynthia Liem at Delft University of Technology.
